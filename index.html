NUMPADMASH a game by galacemiguel
Instructions

Numpad Mash is a game about speed and accuracy. Any number from one to nine will be displayed on-screen at random for a set amount of time. The objective is to press on your keyboard* (or click on the virtual numpad) the corresponding number before it disappears.

Points will be awarded for every correct press and will similarly be deducted for every mistake. Consecutive correct presses will also garner extra points, so make sure to keep a streak up!

You will have 30 seconds to mash away. Good luck!

*Make sure to turn on Num Lock if you're playing on a numpad!
